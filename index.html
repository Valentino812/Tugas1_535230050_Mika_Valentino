Hi, i'm Mika-a Informatics Engineering Student and Photographer based in Jakarta, Indonesia

With deep passion for both technology and photography, I decide to specialize in both worlds. My journey in photography started since I was a child. It all starts with my grandfather’s old analog film camera, the Canon EOS AE-1 that he given for me. It is my favorite even to this day, aged like a fine wine. The world of photography has always been magnificent for me, moments captured to be hold forever in a single frame, preserving the emotions, stories, and ethereal sceneries of life. I strive to create images that evoke emotion, whether through urban and natural landscapes, candid shots of everyday life, or my experimental shots.

As an Informatics Engineering student, I am deeply fascinated by how computers work. My academic journey has given me the understanding of how data, algorithms, and systems that can transform complex challenges into innovative solutions. I seek to enter myself to the digital world, to invent solutions, perhaps to contribute in helping my nation’s struggle for progress in technology, or maybe becoming an indie game developer which I have been dreaming to be for a long time. But the road is still long. For now, my website is going to be the archive of my journey in learning.
